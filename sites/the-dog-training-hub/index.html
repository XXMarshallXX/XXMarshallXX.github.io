Start with the essentials

If you are not sure where to begin, focus on a few high-impact routines first:

  • Keep training sessions short, upbeat, and consistent.
  • Reward the behavior you want to see more often.
  • Set your dog up for success with manageable distractions.
  • Practice in real life, not just during formal sessions.
  • Be patient as learning develops through repetition.
